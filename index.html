Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Ummul Qura Science Academy.

Where is Ummul Qura Science Academy located?

Ummul Qura Science Academy is located opposite State Lowcost, Old Kano Road, Azare, Bauchi State, Nigeria.

What educational levels does the academy offer?

The academy offers Nursery, Primary, and Secondary education levels.
